About Us

What we are doing to the forest of the world is but a mirror reflection to ourselves and to one another.

We are international organization

We are leading stroke charity. We deliver stroke services across the world, campaign for better stroke care, invest in research and fundraise to expand our reach to as many stroke survivors as possible.

We provide information, support and advice to help stroke survivors make the best recovery they can. Find out more about our key areas. Our support services have helped hundreds of thousands of people get through one of the most frightening experiences of their lives and build a life after stroke.

Our campaigning has touched the lives of even more people through initiatives like the FAST campaign, developed with the Department of Health. This campaign has been a big success.
